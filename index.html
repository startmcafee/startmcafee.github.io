Tips for Better Online Safety

  1. Keep your antivirus updated.
  2. Use strong, unique passwords.
  3. Enable two-factor authentication.
  4. Avoid suspicious email links.
  5. Use a secure VPN on public Wi-Fi.
  6. Regularly back up important files.
